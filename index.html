PaperMind

The RAG-Multimodal Research Paper Retrieval & Summarization Pipeline is an end-to-end, LLM-driven system for high-precision academic literature discovery and synthesis, leveraging SentenceTransformer embeddings (all-MiniLM-L6-v2 / all-mpnet-base-v2) to semantically encode abstracts and full-text chunks, stored in a FAISS vector index for ultra-fast ANN retrieval. It operates in two stages: Stage 1 indexes pre-cleaned metadata (metadata.json), generates synthetic query-aligned abstracts with OllamaLLM or ChatOpenAI, and retrieves the top-K relevant papers; Stage 2 extracts and chunks full PDF content, embeds them into FAISS for document-level search, and uses DeepSeek or GPT-4-Turbo to generate structured workflows for multi-step research planning. The pipeline includes robust evaluation modules—Ragas aspect critics for factual faithfulness, OpenAI-based context–summary alignment scoring, hybrid QA + embedding similarity checks to validate key research slots (task, domain, model, goal), and T5 log-probability scoring for slot-level truthfulness—enabling scalable, multimodal retrieval-augmented generation that delivers concise, query-specific research insights with measurable alignment to source material.

  • Created - April 2025
  • Tech Stack - Python, FAISS, SentenceTransformers, Hugging Face Transformers, LangChain, OpenAI GPT-4-Turbo, OllamaLLM, DeepSeek LLM, Ragas, PyTorch, scikit-learn, Pandas, PyMuPDF, JSON
